Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Smart Digital Calendar

What is the Smart Digital Calendar and how does it work?

The Smart Digital Calendar is a 15.6-inch touchscreen wall planner that serves as an all-in-one family organizer. It connects to Wi-Fi and syncs with popular calendar services including Google Calendar, iCloud, Outlook, Yahoo, and Cozi. You can view schedules, assign chores, plan meals, and set reminders all from one central hub. When not in use, it transforms into a 1080p digital photo frame displaying family photos.

Does the Smart Digital Calendar require a subscription?

No subscription fees required. You pay $229 once and get full functionality forever. All features including calendar sync, chore charts, meal planning, reminders, and the digital photo frame are included with no recurring costs. This is a key advantage over competitors like Skylight Calendar which require monthly subscriptions.

What calendars can sync with this digital calendar?

The Smart Digital Calendar syncs with all major calendar platforms: Google Calendar, Apple iCloud, Microsoft Outlook, Yahoo Calendar, and Cozi. All updates sync in real-time across all your devices through the free Zical mobile app. This makes it ideal for families where different members use different calendar systems.

Can I mount the digital calendar on the wall?

Yes, the Smart Digital Calendar offers flexible placement options. You can mount it on the wall or place it on a countertop using the included stand. Its slim, modern design with a white, wood, or black frame fits seamlessly in kitchens, hallways, living rooms, or home offices.

What is the screen size and display quality?

The Smart Digital Calendar features a 15.6-inch IPS HD touchscreen display with 1920x1080 resolution (Full HD). The IPS panel provides wide viewing angles and accurate colors. The touchscreen is responsive and precise for navigating schedules and interacting with the interface.

How does the chore chart feature work?

The interactive chore chart lets you assign tasks to each family member with custom avatars and color-coding. You can track progress, set recurring tasks for things like tidying, pet care, or homework, and motivate kids to participate. The visual system encourages accountability and makes chores fun for children.

How long does setup take?

Setup takes approximately 5 minutes. Simply plug in the device, connect to Wi-Fi, download the free Zical app, and sync your existing calendars. The intuitive interface guides you through adding family members and customizing the display.

What colors/frames are available?

The Smart Digital Calendar is available in three frame colors: White Frame, Wood Frame, and Black Frame. All three options are priced at $229.00 and offer the same features. Choose the color that best matches your home decor.
